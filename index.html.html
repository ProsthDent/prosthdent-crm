Zapier automation — connect your lead sources
Once configured, leads from Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, and your website form will appear here automatically. Give Zapier this webhook URL:
https://prosthdent-crm.netlify.app/api/leads
Fields Zapier should send: fname, lname, phone, email, source, treatment, notes
Step-by-step
  1. Go to zapier.com → Create Zap
  2. Trigger: Facebook Lead Ads → “New Lead” → select your ProsthDent ad account
  3. Action: Webhooks by Zapier → POST → paste the URL above
  4. Map fields: First Name → fname, Last Name → lname, Phone → phone, Email → email. Set source to “Facebook Lead Ads” as a static value.
  5. Test & turn on. Repeat for Instagram Lead Ads, Google Ads, and your website form.
